Story

Graphic design has always been about more than decoration for me; it's about exploring stories and meaning and how they can be contained or passed along by something visible. Letters are a prime example of this. Not only do they speak through sentences and paragraphs, but even one letter by itself can be a picture that says 1000 words on its own. You already know about this. Ever owned something with the first letter of your name on it?

Exploring these ideas lead me to design these unique letterforms that are constructed from simple geometric relationships. The framework of curves and lines behind the letter make visible the many connections a letter can signify: your name, your hometown, your "word of the year."
